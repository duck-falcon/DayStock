‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Where is my data stored?

A: All data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to external servers.

Q: What happens if I set consumption to 0?

A: With 0 consumption, no automatic deduction occurs and remaining days shows "No consumption."

Q: What happens when stock reaches 0?

A: When the app launches and finds items out of stock, it will notify you (if notifications are enabled).

Q: Can I use it without worrying about timing?

A: Yes. It calculates days based on midnight crossings, so it accurately tracks elapsed days regardless of what time you open the app.
