‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Where is my data stored?

A: All data is stored locally on your device. With iCloud sync enabled, you can sync data across multiple devices.

Q: How do I backup my data?

A: Enable iCloud sync for automatic backup. Go to Settings and turn on "iCloud Sync".

Q: Can I customize categories?

A: Yes, you can add, edit, and delete categories from the Settings screen.

Q: I'm not receiving notifications

A: Check that notifications are enabled for DayStock in iOS Settings. Go to Settings > Notifications > DayStock.
