Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 23, 2025

Introduction

Thank you for using DayStock (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles user information and is committed to protecting your privacy.

Information We Collect

The App respects your privacy to the fullest extent and does not collect any personal information.

  • No user account information is collected
  • No location data is collected
  • No usage analytics data is collected
  • No advertising identifiers are collected

Data Storage

All inventory data created and managed within the App is stored locally on your device. This data:

  • Is never transmitted to external servers
  • Is never shared with third parties
  • Is completely removed when deleted from your device

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any analytics, advertising, or other third-party services.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. In fact, the App does not collect personal information from users of any age.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time as necessary. Any significant changes will be notified within the App.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
